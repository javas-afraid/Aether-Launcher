## Contributing
Hello and welcome to contributing to Aether Launcher! We appreciate your interest and are thrilled to have you join our community. Working together, we can enhance the functionality and user experience of this innovative platform. Your skills and ideas are invaluable as we strive to make Aether Launcher a powerful tool for everyone. We encourage you to share your thoughts and suggestions freely, as collaboration is at the core of our mission. Whether you are a seasoned developer or new to the field, your insights can help shape the future of this project for the benefit of all users.

As creators, we prioritize contributing as a source of inspiration and innovation. Our commitment is to not only produce content but also to foster an environment where ideas can flourish and evolve. Below are the main elements that guide our efforts, ensuring that we remain focused and aligned with our goals.

One of the primary aspects we concentrate on is community engagement. Our creators are dedicated to connecting with our audience, listening to their feedback, and responding to their needs. We believe that a strong, supportive community is essential for growth and improvement. By actively participating in discussions and understanding the interests and concerns of users, we can tailor our content to be more relevant and impactful.

Another important aspect is collaboration. We recognize that great ideas often come from working together with others who share our vision. By partnering with fellow creators, we can pool resources and expertise, expanding our horizons and enriching the content we produce. This collaborative spirit not only enhances the quality of our work but also inspires creativity that might not emerge in isolation.

Education and improvement are also key priorities. We are constantly seeking new ways to enhance our skills and knowledge. Through workshops, tutorials, and shared experiences, we empower ourselves and each other to push the boundaries of what we can create. This commitment to lifelong learning strengthens our community and ensures that we stay at the forefront of our craft.

Consistency in quality is another cornerstone of our approach. We strive to maintain high standards in all our contributions. This means that every piece of content we share is thoughtfully crafted, whether it's a video, an article, or a piece of artwork. We respect our audience's time and trust, and we want to deliver work that they find valuable and engaging.

Lastly, it is essential for us to celebrate the diversity of our creators. Every individual brings unique perspectives and experiences, which enrich our collective output. By embracing this diversity, we create a more vibrant and inclusive environment that reflects the broad spectrum of human creativity. It allows us to reach out to a wider audience and connect with people from various backgrounds, ensuring that our contributions resonate on multiple levels.

All users engaged with our platform are represented. This representation is vital because it ensures that everyone feels valued and recognized within our community. By taking these key elements seriously, we work towards creating a collaborative ecosystem that thrives on collective growth and shared successes.

As launcher developers, security, thread stability, and user data privacy are our absolute highest priorities. We do not care about a perfect wall of green GitHub squares if the code underneath compromises user safety or introduces race conditions into the file downloader.
We explicitly approve and encourage developers to spend days planning, debugging, and testing their code locally without committing until a feature is genuinely stable. We will reject any Pull Request that breaks down logical features into tiny, meaningless daily commits solely intended to farm a streak metric. Quality, safety, and a smooth launch process will always take precedence over algorithmic vanity metrics. We recognize that a solid wall of green squares looks impressive, but we value code depth, architectural integrity, and developer well-being over algorithmic vanity metrics.

Contributions that attempt to force granular, meaningless commits just to fulfill a daily streak requirement will be closed. We encourage users to use this launcher to build a structured, high-quality project at a healthy pace, treating the streak as a byproduct of natural progress rather than the primary goal.
